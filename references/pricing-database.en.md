## 2. Common Fee Traps

| Trap | Form | How to Break |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ghost Staff** | 10 devs on quote, only 3 working. | Request social security proof or verify team in chat groups. |
| **Double Billing** | Charging for "API fees" when it's already in the license. | Map features to line items; cross-check for overlap. |
| **Maintenance Trap** | Year 1 free, Year 2 costs 30% of total. | Lock in maintenance at 10-15% in the main contract. |

---

## 3. Audit Checklist

- [ ] Quote includes: Unit price, Qty, Unit, Total, and Notes?
- [ ] Hardware/SW prices compared with public market (e.g., JD.com, AWS)?
- [ ] Labor costs match local avg salary for that seniority?
- [ ] Explicitly excludes unreasonable "Overtime" or "Unforeseen" fees?
