# Industry Knowledge & Environment Checklist (EN)

This document provides environment checklists for different industries, identifying hidden risks across Technical, Policy, Supply Chain, and Market dimensions.

---

## 1. Environment Categories

| Dimension | Focus | Common Overlooked Points |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Technical** | Infrastructure, Compatibility, Scalability | Legacy data cleaning costs, private protocol limits, server scaling tiers |
| **Policy** | Entry permits, Data compliance, Local subsidies | Temporary production limits, cross-border data approvals, annual license renewals |
| **Supply Chain** | Lead times, Vendor dependency, Logistics | Single-source risks, raw material price pass-through, custom part sampling cycles |
| **Market** | Competitors, User habits, Channel barriers | Sudden price wars, platform algorithm black boxes, entry fees & payment terms |

---

## 2. Sector-Specific Checklists

### E-commerce & Digital
- [ ] **Tech**: Legacy system migration? (Dirty data can add 30%+ workload)
- [ ] **Tech**: 3rd-party APIs (Payment, Maps, SMS) registered and verified?
- [ ] **Policy**: App/Mini-program filed? Sensitive data (Finance/Health) involved?
- [ ] **Market**: App store specific qualification requirements (e.g., ICP, licenses)?

### Manufacturing & Production Lines
- [ ] **Tech**: Factory power capacity sufficient for simultaneous operation?
- [ ] **Policy**: Equipment import tariffs or anti-dumping investigations?
- [ ] **Supply Chain**: Maintenance parts available locally within 24h?
- [ ] **Env**: Foundation load-bearing and seismic ratings for precision equipment?

## 3. Cross-Industry Knowledge Tags

The following tags are matched automatically based on input:
- `#DataCompliance`: Privacy, cross-border data.
- `#HardwareConstraint`: Space, power, load, safety.
- `#SupplyChainDependency`: Long lead times, single source.
- `#LicensingRedline`: Educational, Medical, or Special Equipment permits.
- `#AmbiguityAlert`: Vague terms like "optimize" or "improve" without baselines.
