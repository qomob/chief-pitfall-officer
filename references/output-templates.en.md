| Risk Scenario | Trigger Threshold | Recovery SOP (Plan B) | Owner |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Project Delay** | Critical path delay > 3 days | Start 24h shifts/Increase prefab ratio | Project Manager |

| **Vendor Default** | Core materials not arriving on time | Switch to backup brands/Enforce penalty clauses | Procurement |
