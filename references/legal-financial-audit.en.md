# Legal & Financial Audit Standards (EN)

This document defines audit standards for Legal Compliance and Financial Settlement, uncovering "word games" in contracts and "hidden leverage" in payments.

---

## 1. Legal Compliance Audit

### A. Ownership & Rights
- **IP Traps**: Beware clauses stating "outputs based on vendor prototypes belong to the vendor."
  - **Standard**: Explicitly state "All outputs (source code, drafts, docs, data) belong to the Client upon payment."
- **Subcontracting**: Prevent unauthorized pass-through to unqualified teams.
  - **Standard**: Contracts must prohibit subcontracting unless approved in writing by the Client.

### B. Liability & Exit
- **Liability Caps**: Vendors often set caps like "not exceeding 30% of paid amount."
  - **Standard**: For core risks (Data, Safety), ensure higher caps or require Professional Liability Insurance.
- **Exit Mechanism**: Does the Client have the right to "cut losses"?
  - **Standard**: Stipulate "If milestones fail, Client can terminate unilaterally and recover X% of payments."

---

## 2. Financial Settlement Audit

### A. Payment Milestones
- **Imbalance Risk**: High down-payments (e.g., >50%) reduce vendor motivation.
  - **Standard**: Recommended 2-3-3-2 (20% Advance, 30% Demo, 30% Pilot, 20% Final).
- **Metric-Linked**: Payments must link to "Quantifiable Metrics," not just "Time."

### B. Tax & Invoicing
- **Hidden Tax**: Quotes not specifying tax status, leading to later surcharges.
  - **Standard**: Quotes must specify "Inclusive of VAT (Rate X%)." No private or cash transfers.

---

## 3. Legal & Financial Checklist

- [ ] **Legal**: Includes Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and Anti-Bribery Clause?
- [ ] **Legal**: Dispute resolution venue set at Client's location?
- [ ] **Financial**: Verified vendor's tax status (VAT payer)?
- [ ] **Financial**: Stipulated "Final settlement based on Client/3rd-party audit"?
