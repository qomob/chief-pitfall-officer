# F&B/Retail Industry Adaptation Guide

### Typical Scenarios
- Brand Image Upgrade (VI+SI)
- New Store Opening SOP
- Menu/Product Line Adjustment & Pricing
- Delivery Business Setup & Operations
- Chain Store Standardization

### Selection Decision Tree
```
[Demand Type]
├── Image Upgrade
│   ├── Budget < 100k RMB → Light Renovation (Soft deco + Lighting + VI)
│   ├── 100k - 300k RMB → Medium Renovation (Facade + Dining area + VI)
│   └── Budget > 300k RMB → Full Overhaul (VI + SI + Equipment + Marketing)
├── New Store Opening
│   ├── Single Store → Standard SOP (Site → Design → Const → Hiring → Opening)
│   └── Flagship/First Store → Template building + Pilot + Replication model
└── Ops Optimization
    ├── Online-focused → Delivery system setup
    └── Offline-focused → Dining flow optimization + Turnover rate improvement
```

### Audit Priorities
Space design, Supply chain, Compliance approvals, Customer experience

### Environment Checkpoints
- **Policy**: Signage height/brightness vs. local City Management visual rules.
- **Policy**: Kitchen drainage/exhaust vs. Environmental Protection certification.
- **Supply Chain**: At least 2 stable suppliers for core ingredients.
- **Market**: Benchmarking turnover rates & delivery ratios of competitors in the area.

### Participant Risk Warnings
- **Role**: Construction Lead. **Risk**: Low-ball bidding followed by hidden change orders.
- **Role**: Designer. **Risk**: Design specs mismatch site dimensions, leading to rework.
- **Prevention**: All design changes must be signed off by a 3rd-party supervisor; keep raw site measurement data.

### Key Acceptance Items
- **Functional**: POS stress test (100 tx/min without dropping).
- **Performance**: Exhaust noise ≤ 65dB at max power.
- **Compliance**: Food-grade certification for renovation materials.

### Execution Blueprint (Store Renovation Edition)

#### 1. Pre-launch Survey (Client Self-check)
- [ ] **Property Docs**: Structural, plumbing, and electrical drawings.
- [ ] **Capacity**: Power rating and exhaust pipe diameter vs. new equipment needs.
- [ ] **Environment**: Mall/Street rules for material transport and waste removal.

#### 2. Vendor Evaluation Standards
- **Qualifications (20%)**: Grade II Decoration, Grade II Fire Safety.
- **Experience (40%)**: 3+ similar F&B projects in the last 2 years.
- **Timeline (20%)**: Prefab capability; 7-15 day deadline compliance.
- **Support (20%)**: Local maintenance with 2-hour response time.

#### 3. Milestones & Inspection
- **Kickoff**: Site verification vs. design.
- **Plumbing/Electrical (★ Core Audit)**: 0.8Mpa pressure test (30min); wire brand/spec audit.
- **Carpentry**: Joist density and panel edge treatment inspection.
- **Installation**: Signage stability and POS interface verification.

#### 4. Compliance & Licensing
- **Fire Safety**: Mandatory filing for spaces > 300㎡.
- **Environmental**: Smoke purifier installation and cleaning logs.
- **Signage**: City Management rendering approval before hoarding.

#### 5. Launch Checklist (Final 100 Meters)
- **Engineering**: Formaldehyde treatment; 4-hour full-load power test.
- **Operational**: UI updates; POS stress test; staff service drills.
- **Legal**: Display Business License and Food Permit on-site.
